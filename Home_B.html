ABOUT US

Beneficial Life Insurance Co., Inc. (BenLife) is a wholly Filipino-owned life insurance company established on March 16, 1960. From an initial authorized capitalization of P2.5 million, it now has over P1.11 billion in paid up capital, P4.08 billion in total equity and over P9.86 billion in consolidated assets (based on audited consolidated financial statements as of December 31, 2022).


BenLife’s commitment to quality customer service is manifested through its real-time and highly received text-SMS with voice customer-linkage system, and a caring service staff that endeavors to live by BenLife’s service philosophy, “AlagangTunay, AlagangBenLife.”
