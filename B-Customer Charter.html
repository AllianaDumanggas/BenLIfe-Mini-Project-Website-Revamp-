Customer Charter

WELCOME TO BENLIFE’S CUSTOMER CHARTER

All of us at BenLife aim to provide quality, efficient, responsive, and reliable service to our customers. We are always ready to listen, help, and advise the best way we can…

OUR COMMITMENT TO YOU – OUR CUSTOMERS

We have set a standard commitment on responsibilities towards you and will strive towards upholding it.

BenLife is committed to:

Know You- our customer, and understand your needs and preferences, and help you find the right product to suit your needs; Provide our customers with clear and complete information to give them an in-depth understanding of our insurance products and keep them appropriately informed at all times; Protect and respect our customers’ privacy and confidentiality of personal information; Provide professional customer service and prompt response to inquiries and complaints.

FEEDBACK AND COMPLAINTS

Your feedback is important to us.

To ensure strong customer service and customer satisfaction, your feedback, experience and concerns are highly appreciated. Whether you would like to provide a suggestion, pay a compliment, or make a complaint, your feedback allows us to monitor whether we are meeting your expectations and to help us to continuously strive to improve our service.
